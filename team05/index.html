Group Leader

Wu Qiong

Ph.D.

Assistant prof.

Wu Qiong, graduated from Wuhan University,whose research direction is nucleic acid nanotechnology and biomedical diagnosis and treatment applications, mainly developing new methods based on functional nucleic acid nanomaterials for early diagnosis of major diseases. In the past five years, she has published 14 papers, including 6 SCI papers as the first author, with a total citation rate of more than 200 times. She was selected as "Chutian Scholar (Chutian Student)" of Hubei Province high-level Talent Plan. At present, she is presiding over the national Natural Science Foundation of China and the Youth project of Hubei Provincial Health Commission.
